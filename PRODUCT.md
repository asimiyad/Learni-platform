# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Teachers and students using a gamified learning ecosystem. Teachers create, manage, and assign interactive lessons, track student progress, and manage rewards. Students complete lessons, earn points/badges, maintain streaks, and unlock rewards. Both share the platform daily as part of the school workflow.

## Product Purpose

Learni is a gamified lesson platform that makes classroom engagement interactive. Teachers build multimedia lessons with quizzes, templates, and AI generation. Students learn through structured content with progress tracking, points, badges, and reward systems. Success means students are motivated to learn and teachers save time creating content.

## Brand Personality

Modern, Clean, Trustworthy. Professional educational tool that feels approachable, not corporate. Warm without being childish. Calm without being dull.

## Anti-references

- Overly childish "edutainment" UIs with cartoon mascots and loud colors
- Dense enterprise LMS dashboards (Moodle, Blackboard)
- Boring SaaS dashboards with gray cards on gray backgrounds
- Purple-to-blue gradient AI tells

## Design Principles

1. **Confidence through clarity.** Every screen answers "what am I looking at and what should I do next?"
2. **Progress is visible.** Gamification elements (points, streaks, badges) are always glanceable but never noisy.
3. **Teacher tool, student experience.** Teacher surfaces prioritize efficiency; student surfaces prioritize delight and motivation.
4. **Consistency builds trust.** Same component vocabulary across every screen. No surprises.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

- WCAG AA minimum (4.5:1 body text contrast)
- Reduced motion support via prefers-reduced-motion
- Clear focus indicators on all interactive elements
- Meaningful link text (no "click here")
